====== Creating New Facilities ======

The following directions cover how to create new facilities in the Sahana Agasti disaster management system.

{{:facilities.png|}}


===== Step 1 =====

Enter a name in the "Name" field.

===== Step 2 =====

Provide the new facility's alphanumeric identifier code.

===== Step 3 =====

Designate which resources are available at the facility. Multiple resources can be assigned to a facility. For example a site may provide both a evacuation center and a special medical needs shelter. 

===== Step 4 =====

Each site should have a point of contact assigned. Fill out the contact information fields for who is responsible for managing the facility physical building.

===== Step 5 =====

Enter the address information for the facility.

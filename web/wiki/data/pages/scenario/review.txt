====== Scenario Creator Walk-Through ======

===== Review =====
The scenario review page is available to emergency managers from the Scenario List and at the conclusion of the Scenario Creation Wizard. From here users will be able to view summary statistics regarding their scenarios, jump to a step in the wizard to edit the scenario, and deploy this scenario as an event.

To jump to any step in the Scenario Wizard click the name of the step.

{{:scenario:scenarioreview.png?700|Scenario Review}}

  * To Deploy the scenario as an event click **Deploy as Event**.
  * If your organization has multiple response plans you'll want to have a scenario for each plan.  Click the **Create Another Scenario** button.

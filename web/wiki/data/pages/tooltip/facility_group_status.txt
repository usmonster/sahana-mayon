===== Facility Group Status =====

At the time of activation a facility group can be in different statuses to organization activation:
  * **Available** - Could be activated and used for deployment.
  * **Standby** - Standing by for activation.
  * **Active** - Active and in use.
  * **Closed** - Closed.  No longer available.
  * **Unavailable** - Unavailable for some reason.

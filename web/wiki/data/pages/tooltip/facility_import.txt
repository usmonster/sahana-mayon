===== Facility Import =====

To import facilities, click the "Import Facilities" link.
**Facilities are uploaded as part of a scenario.** The related scenario **must** be created before facilities are imported.

==== To Import Facilities ====
  - Download the [[manual:user:facilities&#facility_import|Facility Specification Sheet]]
  - Enter information on your facilities.  If you want help, see the Facility Tutorial's [[manual:user:facilities:import|Facility Import Step]]
  - Click "Facility Import"
  - Click "Browse" and enter the location of the import file on your computer
  - Select the Scenario to upload to
  - Click "Upload"
